About Me My Research Teaching Other Activities

I'm a postdoctoral fellow at UNSW Sydney, hosted by Toby Walsh. I'm a member of the ADT group at the School of Computer Science and Engineering. I do research on topics in Computational Social Choice. Before coming to Sydney I was a PhD candidate at the University of Amsterdam, supervised by Ulle Endriss. I received my MSc in Logic from the University of Amsterdam in 2016. Even longer before that (in 2014), I graduated from the University of California, Berkeley with a BA in Cognitive Science. When I'm not studying formal models of collective decision making, I like to knit and solve crossword puzzles. (This homepage is still a work in progress, if you notice something missing or wrong, I absolutely know about it for sure...but feel free to still let me know.)
